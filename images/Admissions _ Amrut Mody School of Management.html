Admissions

We aim to bring curious, insightful minds dedicated to the spirit of enquiry to the school. We seek to create an intellectually diverse, gender-balanced student pool by admitting students across geographies, cultures and socio-economic class.

For more information on the admissions process for each programme, see below.

 

Undergraduate Admissions

Admissions to undergraduate and integrated programmes at AMSOM are co-ordinated by the Office of Undergraduate Admissions and Financial Aid, Ahmedabad University. We seek to register individuals of diverse talents and exceptional self-motivation, focused not only on building excellent careers but also on serving their communities.

Admissions to BBA (Hons), BCom (Hons), BCom Professional (Hons), Integrated MBA are open now.

Doctoral Programme Admissions 2019

Ahmedabad University encourages high quality research through doctoral degree programmes.  The doctoral degree programme at the Amrut Mody School of Management offers an inter-disciplinary exposure to research.  School of Management is actively involved in a process of building networks and partnerships with leading institutions enabling scholars to work with peers across the world.
